The best blush ties your whole look together just like a present that's wrapped with a bow. Let's find out which blush is the perfect color for you:

Start with choosing a shade that shares the same undertone as you. Rose and berry blushes flatter deeper complexions. For medium to tan skin tones, mauve or peach blushes provide a healthy flush. Pink blushes add a pop of color to complexions that range from fair to light.
